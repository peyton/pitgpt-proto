Usage Guidelines
✓ Do
  • Use logo.svg for app icons, favicons, and standalone placements ≥ 32px
  • Use the header logomark (cropped viewBox, no container) inline with the wordmark in navigation
  • Maintain the 6px gap between logomark and wordmark in header contexts
  • Keep "Pit" in gray-900 and "GPT" in pink-500 for the wordmark
  • On dark backgrounds, shift "GPT" to pink-300 and "Pit" to white
✗ Don't
  • Don't display the full logo.svg (with container square) below 32px — use the header logomark instead
  • Don't rotate, skew, or add drop shadows to the logo
  • Don't change the circle gradient colors or opacity values
  • Don't separate the two circles — the overlap is the concept
  • Don't place on backgrounds that clash with the pink gradient (orange, red, magenta)

Design Principles
◉

Bold Simplicity

One typeface. One accent color. Strip away everything that doesn't earn its place. The interface disappears so the science becomes obvious.

◠

Progressive Disclosure

Show the answer first, then the evidence, then the methodology. Statistical details live behind (i) tooltips — accessible but never in the way.

◇

Honest Uncertainty

Always show confidence intervals, not just point estimates. Always show caveats, not just results. Designed to prevent overconfidence in personal data.

▷

Conversational Entry

You don't "configure an experiment" — you ask a question. The system meets you where you are and handles the science behind the scenes.
