# How to compare two hair masks without fooling yourself

Synthetic benchmark document for PitGPT v1 evaluation.
Source type: guide
Use: deterministic fixture for research-ingestion tests.

Use one hair mask for a full week, then switch the next week. Track softness the day after wash, visible frizz, and scalp irritation. Keep shampoo, conditioner, styling products, and wash frequency constant.

This guide is methodological. It does not establish that any specific mask works.
