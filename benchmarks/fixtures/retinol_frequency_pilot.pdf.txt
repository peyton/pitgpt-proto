# Pilot comparison of retinol frequency and visible smoothness

Synthetic benchmark document for PitGPT v1 evaluation.
Source type: PDF abstract
Use: deterministic fixture for research-ingestion tests.

Methods: adults with self-described dull skin compared retinol use 1 night per week versus 2 nights per week over 8 weeks.

Outcomes: skin smoothness, visible flaking, stinging, and redness.
Results: visible smoothness improved more in the twice-weekly arm, but irritation was also higher.
Interpretation: moderate evidence for a tradeoff between effect and tolerability.
