# Small split-head pilot of rice water rinse on visible frizz

Synthetic benchmark document for PitGPT v1 evaluation.
Source type: PDF abstract
Use: deterministic fixture for research-ingestion tests.

Methods: 12 adults with self-reported frizz used a rice water rinse on one side of the scalp and water only on the other for 4 weeks.

Outcome: morning frizz score from 0 to 10.
Results: difference was small and variable. Some participants improved, some worsened. No serious adverse events.
Interpretation: preliminary and weak evidence; effect likely heterogeneous.
